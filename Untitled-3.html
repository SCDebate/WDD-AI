About Us

The Young Marines is a youth education and service program for boys and girls, ages 8 through the completion of high school. The program promotes the mental, moral, and physical development of its members. The Young Marines is the focal point for the Marine Corps' Youth Drug Demand Reduction efforts.

Our program emphasizes the importance of teamwork, leadership, and community service. Members participate in a variety of activities designed to build character and instill values that will serve them throughout their lives.

Our Mission

The mission of the Young Marines is to positively impact America's future by providing quality youth development programs for boys and girls that nurture and develop its members into responsible citizens who enjoy and promote a healthy, drug-free lifestyle.

We aim to instill a sense of pride and responsibility in our members, encouraging them to become active and engaged citizens. Our programs are designed to challenge and inspire young people, helping them to reach their full potential.

Programs

The Young Marines offers a variety of programs and activities, including leadership schools, encampments, and community service projects. These programs are designed to instill the core values of leadership, teamwork, and discipline in its members.

Our programs include:

  • Leadership Training: Courses and workshops that focus on developing leadership skills and qualities.
  • Physical Fitness: Activities and challenges that promote physical health and well-being.
  • Community Service: Projects that encourage members to give back to their communities.
  • Educational Workshops: Sessions on topics such as first aid, public speaking, and financial literacy.

Leadership Schools

The Young Marines offers several leadership schools that provide advanced training for members who demonstrate exceptional leadership potential. These schools focus on developing skills such as public speaking, team building, and problem-solving.

Our leadership schools are designed to challenge and inspire our members, helping them to develop the confidence and skills needed to lead effectively. These schools include:

  • Junior Leadership School: An introductory program for younger members that focuses on basic leadership principles and skills.
  • Senior Leadership School: An advanced program for older members that includes more complex leadership challenges and scenarios.
  • National Leadership Academy: A prestigious program that brings together top Young Marines from across the country for intensive leadership training and development.
