Welcome to the Young Marines

The Young Marines is a youth education and service program for boys and girls, ages 8 through the completion of high school. The program promotes the mental, moral, and physical development of its members. The Young Marines was founded in 1958 in Waterbury, Connecticut.

Programs

  • Leadership Schools
  • National Encampments
  • Community Service Projects
